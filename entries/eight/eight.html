Scent Cloud

fragments of smell drift in the dark:
甘さ、金属、蒸気、髪、
and all the notes that never fit
on a bottle’s tiny label.

burnt sugar
wet concrete
skin heat
電車のブレーキ
cold jasmine
chlorine
old paper
laundry steam
soil after rain
夜の髪
synthetic rose
metallic air
